Fuel Your Progress

You can’t out-train a poor diet. Good food = good fuel. Whether you're trying to lose fat or gain lean muscle, your results start with what's on your plate. Here's how to simplify your nutrition.

How to Track Calories

Step 1: Use a free app like MyFitnessPal or Chronometer.
Step 2: Know your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).
Step 3: Use a food scale or nutrition label for accuracy.
Step 4: Be consistent — it’s better to be 80% accurate than 0%.
