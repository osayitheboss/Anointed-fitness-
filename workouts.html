Start Moving

Whether you're starting from zero or picking it back up, movement is medicine. Below you'll find a sample week of beginner workouts you can do from home — no gym, no excuses.

Weekly Workout Plan

DayFocusWorkout
MondayFull BodyBodyweight circuit (push-ups, squats, planks)
TuesdayCardioJog/walk 20 mins or 100 jumping jacks
WednesdayCoreCrunches, leg raises, Russian twists
ThursdayStrengthDumbbell rows, push-ups, lunges
FridayStretch + RestFull body stretching or yoga

Tips for Success

✔️ Start slow and listen to your body.
✔️ Progress = consistency, not perfection.
✔️ Warm up and cool down always.
✔️ Hydrate like your muscles depend on it (they do).
